DERM EDGE Clinic Management System
User Manual
Overview
The DERM EDGE Clinic Management System is a centralized digital platform that replaces manual paper logs at the clinic on Tionko Avenue, Davao City. It automates patient registration, clinical medical history, real-time inventory tracking, and billing processing.

System Features
1. Reception & Patient Registration
Purpose: Replaces physical logbooks with a digital patient database registry.

How to Use: Search for existing records by last name, or click "Create New Patient" to register demographic and contact details, then click "Check-In" to queue the patient.

2. Clinical Consultation & Treatment Records
Purpose: Tracks long-term skin health profiles and ongoing aesthetic treatments securely.

How to Use: Select a checked-in patient, review past consultation logs, type in active procedural or medical notes, and click "Save Medical Record".

3. Service & Product Menu Matrix
Purpose: Displays categorized clinical dermatological treatments, advanced skincare services, and aesthetic procedures.

How to Use: Filter the clinical menu by category and click "Quick Add to Bill" to assign services or products to the patient's checkout file.

4. Billing & Invoice Desk
Purpose: Consolidates treatment charges and automatically calculates subtotals, promotional discounts, and local tax requirements.

How to Use: Review the invoice summary itemizing services and retail products, apply discount codes from the menu dropdown, and select "Proceed to Payment".

5. Real-Time Inventory Tracker
Purpose: Automatically deducts product stock balances during sales or procedures to prevent stockouts and over-purchasing.

How to Use: Open "Inventory Management" to monitor batch counts. To alter quantities manually after shipments, select a product, click "Adjust Stock", and input the new value.

System Requirements
Software: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari.

Hardware: Desktop terminals (reception), tablets/laptops (clinical rooms), and a stable internet connection.

Troubleshooting
Page or Bill Not Loading: Reload the browser page and check local Wi-Fi router connectivity.

Stock Balances Inaccurate: Refresh the page to verify that the target transaction was fully finalized at the payment desk.

Slow Search Queries: Clear the browser cache and ensure the browser is updated to its latest version.
